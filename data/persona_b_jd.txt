Supply Chain Operations Manager

We are seeking a Supply Chain Operations Manager to lead our logistics operations and drive data-informed decision-making.

Required Skills:
- Supply Chain Management: Oversee end-to-end supply chain operations including procurement, logistics, and distribution
- ERP Systems: Proficiency with enterprise resource planning platforms (SAP, Oracle, or similar)
- Advanced Excel & Power BI: Create dashboards, reports, and data visualizations for stakeholder presentations
- Cross-functional Leadership: Lead teams across departments, manage managers, and drive organizational change
- Budget Management: Own P&L for operations division, manage budgets, and optimize cost structures
- Vendor Management: Negotiate contracts, manage supplier relationships, and ensure procurement compliance
- Analytics: Use data analytics to identify trends, optimize processes, and make strategic decisions
- Inventory Management: Oversee multi-location inventory systems and optimize stock levels

Nice to Have:
- OSHA safety certification
- Forklift operation experience
- Experience with warehouse management systems
- Procurement and contract negotiation background
