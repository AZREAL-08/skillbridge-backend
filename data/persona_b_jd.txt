Supply Chain Operations Manager — Meesho, Bengaluru

About the Role:
Meesho is India's largest social commerce platform with 140M+ customers and a network of
1,200+ delivery partners. We are hiring a Supply Chain Operations Manager to own the
analytics, vendor strategy, and cross-functional execution for our regional fulfillment network.

This is a step up from floor-level operations — you will be managing managers, owning P&L,
and driving decisions from data rather than instinct.

Responsibilities:
- Own end-to-end supply chain performance for the South India fulfillment cluster (4 facilities)
- Build and maintain operational dashboards in Power BI for weekly leadership reviews
- Lead ERP migration from legacy WMS to SAP S/4HANA for the regional cluster
- Manage vendor contracts for 30+ logistics partners; negotiate SLAs and rate cards
- Develop and own the annual operations budget (~₹40Cr); track actuals vs forecast monthly
- Use supply chain analytics to identify demand patterns, reduce stockouts, and optimize routing
- Lead a team of 4 Operations Leads (each managing 15–30 associates)
- Drive cross-functional initiatives with tech, finance, and commercial teams

Required Skills:
- ERP Systems: Hands-on experience with SAP (MM/WM/S4HANA) or Oracle SCM — beyond data entry
- Advanced Excel & Power BI: Build dashboards, write DAX measures, create executive-level reports
- Supply Chain Analytics: Demand forecasting, inventory optimization, cost-per-shipment analysis
- Cross-functional Leadership: Experience managing leads or managers, not just associates
- Budget Management: Ownership of operational P&L; variance analysis and cost optimization
- Vendor Management: Contract negotiation, SLA management, supplier performance reviews
- Inventory Management: Multi-location inventory strategy, safety stock modeling, ABC analysis

Nice to Have:
- OSHA or safety certification background
- Warehouse operations hands-on experience (strong plus for credibility with floor teams)
- Familiarity with Python or SQL for ad-hoc data queries
- Six Sigma Green Belt or equivalent process improvement certification
